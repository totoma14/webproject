3 EASY STEPS TO LET PROPERTY

Advertising your property on ParcHouse.com is quick, simple and secure. Just let us know a few details about your property, and your ad is ready to go. We will automatically list your advert, and Rightmove and Zoopla, making it visible to thousands of potential tenants instantly.

We’ll handle all of your enquiries for you, and you will conduct the viewings. All you need to do is confirm a suitable time, and you then meet the potential tenant at the property to show them around. If you’re not available to conduct the viewing yourself, we can manage them for you.

If your potential tenant wants to secure your property, and you’re happy to accept them, you must then refer them back to us to process the initial part of the booking. We can also organise tenancy agreements, collect deposits, or completely manage your property if you need our help.
