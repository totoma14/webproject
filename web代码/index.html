WELCOME

Welcome to a new kind of student living experience, designed with you in mind. We are very good choice for student accommodation because we genuinely care about how you want to live. Living with Parc House isn't just about buildings, it’s about safe, secure homes where you can study and socialise in comfort.

Our properties are perfectly located near city centres or Singleton and Bay campus, and come fully equipped with everything you need, including free wi-fi and contents insurance.

WHY PARC HOUSE

Great facilities and locations close to city centres and University campuses. Very Easy to access to public transportation.

Our staff and maintenance group serve all our student and landlord clients 24 hours, 7 days a week including holidays.

We have CCTV and conduct regular safety reviews of all our houses supported by a robust fire risk assessment regime.
